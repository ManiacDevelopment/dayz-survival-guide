Base Building

A base is never perfectly safe. Build smart, hide better and never store everything in one place.

Tools Walls & gates Location Stashes

Tools

You need tools such as hatchets, saws, shovels, hammers, pliers, nails, wire and combination locks depending on your build.

Walls & gates

Walls protect storage but also advertise that loot exists. Gates need wire and pliers. Combination locks are essential but not permanent protection.

Location

Avoid obvious barns, police stations and high-traffic military routes. Remote bases survive longer, but hidden stashes often survive longest.

Stashes

Split important gear between buried stashes, crates and alternate locations. Do not keep all weapons, medicine and tools in one base.
