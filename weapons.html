Weapons

Weapons Guide

The best weapon is the one you have ammo for, can control and know when not to fire.

Melee Firearms Ammo Attachments

Melee

Melee weapons are critical early game. They save ammo and help you deal with infected quietly.

Firearms

Pistols are useful early, rifles dominate open areas and automatic weapons are strongest in close fights. Do not shoot unless you accept that others may hear you.

Ammo

A gun without matching ammo or magazines is dead weight. Prioritize weapons you can actually feed and maintain.

Attachments

Optics, suppressors and magazines can change a weapon completely. Suppressors are powerful but still not silent to nearby players.
