Melee

  • Melee keeps you quiet and saves ammunition. Keep a reliable tool or weapon for infected.
  • Do not trade hits with several infected in open space.

Pistols

  • Pistols are emergency tools. Suppressed pistols are useful for infected control but can still reveal you nearby.

Rifles and shotguns

  • Shotguns are brutal close range but loud. Rifles reward patience, angles and ammunition discipline.
  • A lower-tier gun with ammunition is better than a rare rifle with an empty magazine.

Attachments

  • Optics, suppressors, lights and weapon repair kits change how a weapon performs.
  • Do not carry attachments for guns you are unlikely to use.
