Loot Guide

Good loot is not just rare guns. The best loot keeps you alive long enough to use them.

Priority loot Medical Military Storage

Priority loot

  • Knife or sharp tool
  • Bandages or disinfected rags
  • Water container
  • Warm clothing
  • Food source

Medical loot

Hospitals and clinics can contain tetracycline, vitamins, bandages, saline, blood bags and chlorine tablets. Medical gear is often more valuable than another weapon.

Military loot

Military areas contain better weapons, armor and ammunition, but they attract geared players. Approach slowly, listen first and leave if the area feels wrong.

Storage

Backpacks help, but too much gear reduces stamina. Carry what supports your plan, not every item you see.
