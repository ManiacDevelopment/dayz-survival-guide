Vehicles

Vehicles Guide

Cars are freedom, storage and risk. They are loud, valuable and attract attention fast.

Required parts Fuel & water Repairs Safety

Required parts

Most vehicles need wheels, battery, spark plug and radiator. Missing or ruined parts can make the vehicle useless.

Fuel & water

Fuel goes in the tank. Water goes in the radiator. Driving without radiator water can destroy the engine.

Repairs

Carry repair tools when possible. Keep spare parts hidden nearby if you rely on a vehicle.

Safety

Do not park in obvious towns or directly outside your base. Hide vehicles under trees, away from main roads and high-traffic areas.
