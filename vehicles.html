Required parts

  • A working vehicle typically needs correct wheels, battery, spark plug or glow plug depending on vehicle type, radiator where relevant, fuel and water for the radiator.
  • Spark plugs and radiators cannot simply be repaired like some other components, so spares matter.

Fuel and water

  • Fuel runs the vehicle. Water protects the radiator/engine from overheating. Fill before long trips.

Repairs

  • Use the correct repair kits for vehicle parts when possible. Damaged engines, wheels and body panels can turn a trip into a trap.

Security

  • Remove key components when parked if your server rules and setup allow it. Hide vehicles away from common routes.
