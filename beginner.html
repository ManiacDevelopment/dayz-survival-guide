DayZ Beginner Guide

Your first mission is not PvP. Your first mission is to stop dying to thirst, cold, infected and bad route choices.

Survivor rule

Stabilize first, take risks later. Greed kills more runs than patience.

Spawn priorities

  • Check the coastline and road signs to identify your spawn town.
  • Find a water pump before committing inland.
  • Search sheds and small houses for a knife, screwdriver, blade or tool that can cut rags and open food.
  • Do not sprint everywhere until food and water are stable.

Water and food

  • Drink from pumps when possible. Unknown bottles are risky unless emptied and refilled at a safe source.
  • Fruit trees, small village houses, boats, infected clothing and animal hunting can all solve early food.
  • Eat and drink in small amounts if you are sick to reduce vomiting risk.

Infected control

  • Fight one infected at a time. Use doors, fences and buildings to break line of sight.
  • Melee is usually better than wasting ammunition early. Heavy attacks are safer when timed correctly.

Move inland

  • Once you have water, food, a blade and basic warmth, leave the coast.
  • Use treelines and side paths instead of running down the middle of roads.
  • Small inland towns are often safer than coastal chaos and can give enough gear for military zones later.
