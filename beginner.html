DayZ Beginner Guide

Your first goal is not to fight. It is to stabilize, get water, find food, get a blade and move inland alive.

Spawn priorities

When you spawn, check the coast, identify your town, find a water pump and avoid unnecessary fights. Sprinting everywhere burns food and water quickly.

First loot

  • Find a knife, stone knife, screwdriver or sharp tool.
  • Pick up warmer clothes with more storage.
  • Search houses, sheds, boats and small shops.
  • Eat food slowly and drink from safe sources.

Handling infected

Do not fight multiple infected in the open. Use doors, fences and buildings. Sneak when possible and use melee weapons to save ammunition.

Move inland

Once you have water, food and a blade, leave the coast. Inland towns have better loot and fewer fresh spawns. Follow roads carefully, but avoid running directly on them.
