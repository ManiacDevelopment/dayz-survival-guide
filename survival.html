Survival

Survival Guide

Stay warm, hydrated, fed and quiet. Most DayZ deaths start long before the final shot.

Food Water Temperature Health

Food

Fruit, canned food, animals and fishing are reliable food sources. Always use clean hands or gloves when eating after cutting animals.

Water

Water pumps are safest. Unknown bottles can make you sick unless emptied and refilled or treated with chlorine tablets.

Temperature

Wet clothes and cold weather drain your energy. Wring out clothing, make fires when safe and prioritize insulation over style.

Health habits

  • Disinfect rags before bandaging.
  • Avoid drinking unsafe water.
  • Carry tetracycline, vitamins and chlorine tablets.
  • Do not carry too much weight if you need stamina for fights.
