Cholera

  • Cause: dirty water, unsafe bottles or contaminated containers.
  • Treatment: tetracycline or multivitamins. Drink tiny sips and eat tiny bites until stable.

Salmonella / food poisoning

  • Cause: raw meat, rotten food or eating with bloody hands.
  • Treatment: charcoal tablets and/or multivitamins. Stop eating unsafe food and stabilize slowly.

Common cold / influenza

  • Cause: cold, wet clothing, low stats or close contact with sick players.
  • Treatment: warmth, dry clothing, good food/water, tetracycline and multivitamins.

Wound infection

  • Cause: dirty rags, dirty bandages or untreated wounds.
  • Treatment: disinfect early stage. Use tetracycline for later stage infection.

Gas poisoning and chemical exposure

  • Cause: toxic gas zones or unsafe chemical consumption.
  • Treatment: leave exposure first, then use the correct medical item and stabilize blood, food and water.

Kuru / brain prion disease

  • Cause: eating human meat or human fat.
  • Treatment: no practical cure. Prevention is the only reliable answer.
