Cholera

Cause: Dirty water, unsafe bottles or contaminated containers.

Cure: Tetracycline pills. Multivitamins can help your immune system. Drink tiny sips and eat tiny bites to avoid vomiting.

Salmonella / food poisoning

Cause: Raw meat, rotten food, bloody hands or unsafe animal products.

Cure: Charcoal tablets or multivitamins. Eat and drink slowly.

Common cold / influenza

Cause: Being cold, wet, low immunity or close contact with sick survivors.

Cure: Stay warm, dry your clothes, eat, drink, use tetracycline or multivitamins.

Wound infection

Cause: Dirty rags, dirty bandages or letting bleeding heal untreated.

Cure: Early stage: disinfect wound. Later stage: tetracycline. Use clean bandages next time.

Poisoning / chemical sickness

Cause: Toxic zones, chemicals, gasoline, disinfectant or contaminated exposure.

Cure: Charcoal tablets where relevant, clean food/water, leave the toxic area and stabilize stats.

Kuru / brain prion disease

Cause: Eating human meat or human fat.

Cure: No practical cure. Avoid cannibalism unless you accept permanent symptoms.
