Illness Guide

Disease kills slowly. Identify symptoms early, stop the cause and treat before your stats collapse.

Cholera Flu Salmonella Wound infection

Cholera

Usually caused by unsafe water. Symptoms include vomiting after eating or drinking. Drink tiny amounts, eat slowly and use tetracycline. Vitamins can help your immune system.

Flu / cold

Often caused by being cold or wet. Stay warm, dry your clothes, eat and drink carefully and use tetracycline or vitamins.

Salmonella

Usually caused by eating raw meat or eating with bloody hands. Use charcoal tablets and avoid eating large amounts until stable.

Wound infection

Can happen after using dirty rags or untreated bandages. Early stage can be treated with disinfectant. Later stage usually requires tetracycline.
