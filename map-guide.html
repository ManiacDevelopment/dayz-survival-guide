Coast spawns

  • The coast is useful for orientation and early water, but it is unstable because many fresh spawns compete for the same basics.

Inland routes

  • Move through smaller villages, treelines and side roads. Do not follow the most obvious route forever.

Military zones

  • NWAF and Tisy are high-value, high-risk zones. Scout, listen, loot quickly and leave before greed takes over.

Navigation habits

  • Stop in cover before checking maps. Plan both entry and exit. Avoid crossing open fields unless visibility and timing are good.
