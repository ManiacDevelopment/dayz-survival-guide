Map Guide

Navigation is survival. Learn the coast, read landmarks and move inland with a plan.

Coast Inland Military zones Navigation

Coast

The coast is full of fresh spawns, low-tier loot and danger. Use it to stabilize, then leave.

Inland routes

Small villages, hunting areas and isolated buildings are safer routes to better gear. Avoid predictable straight-line movement.

Military zones

Military areas offer strong gear but create PvP hotspots. Scout from distance, listen and never assume the area is empty.

Navigation tips

  • Use power lines, roads, railways and town signs.
  • Compare terrain, water, hills and forests.
  • Travel through tree lines instead of open roads.
  • Plan an exit before entering dangerous areas.
