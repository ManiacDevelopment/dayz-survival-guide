PvP Guide

Most fights are won before the first shot. Sound, patience and positioning matter more than panic aim.

Sound Position During fights After fights

Sound discipline

Footsteps, infected aggro, gunshots and doors reveal players. Stop often and listen before entering towns or military zones.

Positioning

Use cover, elevation and shadows. Avoid skylining yourself on hills and never stand still in obvious windows.

During fights

  • Change position after shooting.
  • Do not loot immediately.
  • Watch for teammates.
  • Use infected chaos to your advantage.

After fights

Heal, reload, listen and relocate. The loudest part of a fight is often the invitation for another player to third-party you.
