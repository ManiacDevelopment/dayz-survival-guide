Sound discipline

  • Footsteps, reloading, eating, doors, infected aggro and inventory movement can expose you. Stop and listen before looting.

Positioning

  • Avoid skylines. Use cover with an exit. Do not stand still in windows after being seen.
  • Rotate after firing if the enemy knows your angle.

Ambush logic

  • Let the target enter the bad position. Shooting too early turns an ambush into a fair fight.
  • Control escape routes when possible.

After the fight

  • Loot fast, prioritize medicine/ammo/armor, then leave. Bodies attract third parties.
